OSWALD
.

Oh, I beg your pardon; I thought you were in the study.

[Comesforward.]

Good-morning, Pastor Manders.

MRS
.

ALVING. Well well, suppose it is. My point is that your judgment as to my married life is founded upon nothing but common knowledge and report.

MANDERS
.

[Softly, so as not to be heard in the dining-room.] Yes; it would not do to provoke scandal.
